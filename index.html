    INTRODUCTION
    ABOUT ACAPAT SOLUTIONS The name ACAPAT is an acronym that means ACADEMIC PATHFINDER, ACA from ACADEMIC, and PAT from PATHFINDER, In essence ACAPAT SOLUTIONS LIMITED exist to pioneer a path that provides solutions that will change the narrative of academics at all levels, raising a new generation of educators that will breed new generation of learners which will in-turn revolutionize the workforce and transform our society in all spheres. ACAPAT SOLUTIONS is an indigenous ICT company with the objective of improving the educational sector through capacity building, content development, innovative concept, and information and communication technology. Our services include: General Educational Consultancy, Training and capacity building, Educational content development, Development of data base Management for schools, installation of ICT and E-learning facilities.

    MILE STONES: ACAPAT SOLUTIONS is the creator of acapat.org, an Academic Social Network platform. The application incorporates e-learning to the Social network platform where all stakeholders across the academia (Students, Tutors, Lecturers, Career Counsellors, and Professionals) can learn, tutor, mentor, interact and build viable relationships towards raising the bar of education at all levels.
    ACAPAT SOLUTIONS Limited has developed an offline Mathematics application called the ACAPAT MATHS X-RAY, based on the NERDC curriculum. The application is currently being used by private Schools across Nigeria.

    ACAPAT SOLUTIONS in collaboration with the Federal Ministry of Education has launched a campaign tagged Breaking Maths-Phobia and Promoting STEM Education, consequently, ACAPAT SOLUTIONS just concluded the training for Mathematics teachers in Federal unity colleges across the six geopolitical zones in Nigeria. ACAPAT SOLUTIONS is a recipient of the SHELL LIVE WIRE AWARD.
